WHAT IS GHOST RAIDER?

I'm glad you asked!


Ghost Raider is a Mobile E-Learning App that teaches to Destiny players, both new and old, how to successfully tackle and complete each of Destiny's four Raids and their Encounters.

VAULT OF GLASS CROTA'S END KING'S FALL WRATH OF THE MACHINE

WHY IS THIS APP USEFUL?

Here's where things get good.


Many Raid Guides online provide substantial detail for newcomers to understand Destiny's Raids, yet most fail to mention mechanics that very well will wipe your team if ignored. Furthermore, there is no up-to-date Mobile E-Learning App for Destiny Raids.

Ghost Raider, which is this App, aims to solve these problems for a Mobile Destiny Audience. From easy-to-understand tutorials to simplistic loot charts, Ghost Raider has all you need to jump feet first in the darkest depths.
